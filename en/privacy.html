Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

HacoStock (the "App") respects your privacy. Our core policy is to collect no personal information whatsoever.

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect or transmit any personal information or usage data. Photos, item information, and settings are stored only on your device.

  • No account registration or login required
  • No analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) are used
  • No ad SDKs are included
  • No automatic crash reports or telemetry are sent

2. Camera & Photo Library Access

The App requests access to the camera and photo library so that you can register item photos. Captured images are stored only on your device and are never sent externally.

3. In-App Purchases

The Pro upgrade uses Apple's in-app purchase system (StoreKit). Payment information is handled by Apple; the App does not access or store payment details.

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

All user data is stored in local storage on your iOS / iPadOS / macOS device. We do not automatically sync to iCloud or any cloud service (this may be offered in the future as an explicit export/import feature).

5. Sharing with Third Parties

Because the App does not collect user data, no data is ever shared with third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

The App can be used by users of any age. Because we do not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated without prior notice. Material changes will be announced on this page.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy, please use our contact form.
