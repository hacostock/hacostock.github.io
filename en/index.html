How it works

Lots of boxes, searchable in seconds.

Snap, sort, search by photo. Three steps replace 30 minutes of digging.

Snap

Just point and shoot. Image recognition adds tags automatically — and OCR also suggests names when there's printed text. Even 100+ items take only seconds each.

Sort

Every box gets an auto-assigned number. Write the same number on the physical box and your real-world storage is bound to the app.

Find

Search by photo: point at a listing photo or any image, and the app pulls up matching items automatically. Or filter by image tags. Find any item even when you have a lot — in seconds.
