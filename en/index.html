Features

📷

Register with a Photo

Snap a photo to register an item instantly. OCR reads the name from the image, and tags are generated automatically.

📦

Organize with Boxes

Group items into "Boxes." Rename them to "Shelf," "Collection," "Category" — whatever fits your style.

🔍

Find Anything Fast

Filter by tags, keywords, favorites, for-sale or sold status. Large collections stay easy to navigate.

💰

Track Sales

Mark each item as "For Sale" or "Sold." Great for resale listings across flea-market platforms.

🎨

Beautiful Design

Five color themes with a glassmorphism UI. Enjoyable just to browse.

🔒

Privacy First

All data is stored on your device. No account required, nothing sent to external servers.
