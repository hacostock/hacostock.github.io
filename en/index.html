How it works

Three steps. No typing.

Snap, store, search. That's the whole flow.

Snap

Just point and shoot. Image recognition adds tags automatically, and OCR captures the name when there's printed text (boxes, book covers, packaging). Even 100+ items take seconds each.

Store

Drop it in a "Box." Rename the concept to Shelf, Collection, Category — whatever fits.

Search

Find anything by tag, keyword, or favorite. Filter for-sale and sold items instantly.

Scenarios

When HacoStock saves the day

Turn every "where did I put that?" into "found it."

01

Sold on a marketplace — wait, where is it?

My figure just sold! …but which box did I put it in? Back of the closet? Drawer?

Match against the listing photo by scrolling the visual grid, or filter by the image-generated tags. See exactly which box it lives in. One tap marks it "Sold" after shipping.

02

"Wait, do I already own this?"

Spotted something at the store. But I have a feeling something similar is already at home…

Check tags or keywords on the spot to avoid duplicate purchases. Your collection stays curated.

03

Know what's in the box — without opening it

Boxes piled in storage. Marker labels like "books" or "misc" only get you so far — you end up opening every one anyway.

Every box in HacoStock gets an auto-assigned number. Write the same number on the cardboard box, and physical meets digital. Tap "Box 3" in the app to see exactly what's inside. Perfect for storage, moving, or off-site units.
