How it works

Three steps. No typing.

Snap, store, search. That's the whole flow.

Snap

Take a photo. OCR reads the name automatically, and tags are generated from the image.

Store

Drop it in a "Box." Rename the concept to Shelf, Collection, Category — whatever fits.

Search

Find anything by tag, keyword, or favorite. Filter for-sale and sold items instantly.
