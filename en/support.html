Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What's the difference between Free and Pro?

The free tier supports up to 3 boxes and 50 items. The Pro upgrade (one-time purchase) unlocks unlimited boxes and items. There is no subscription.

Q. Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. No account is required, and nothing is sent to external servers.

Q. Can I back up my data?

Yes — you can export and import your data from in-app settings. Use this when migrating to a new device.

Q. How do I improve OCR accuracy?

Ensure good lighting and focus on the text area when taking the photo. A clean background also helps OCR work better.

Q. Does it work on iPad and Mac?

Yes — HacoStock runs natively on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Pro purchases are shared across devices using the same Apple ID.

Q. How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Open Settings and tap "Restore Purchases." If you previously purchased Pro under the same Apple ID, it will be re-enabled.

Q. How do I delete items or boxes?

Long-press an item / box, or use the menu in the detail view. Deleted data cannot be restored, so please proceed with care.

If your issue isn't answered above, please use the contact form. Feedback and feature requests are always welcome and inform future updates.
